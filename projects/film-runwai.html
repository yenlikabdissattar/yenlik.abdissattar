AI

RunwAI — Fashion Show

2025

Overview

AI-generated fashion runway combining generative visuals and cinematic editing.

Tools

ComfyUI, AI Image/Video Generation, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator

Process

Garments were designed conceptually, then translated into AI-generated visuals through a structured ComfyUI pipeline. A LoRA-based workflow maintained stylistic consistency across models, outfits, and environments. Outputs were sequenced into a runway-style video, with poster designs extending the project into editorial format.

Reflections

Expanded my understanding of AI as a design tool — building structured workflows that allow creative control within generative systems.
