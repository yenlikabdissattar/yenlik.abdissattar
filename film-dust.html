Film & Motion Graphics

DUST

2025

Overview

Motion graphics exploring texture, grain, and the materiality of moving image.

Tools

Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro

Process

An investigation into the visual language of dust, grain, and surface — using motion as a means to render the invisible visible. Layers of texture are animated to create a sense of atmospheric depth and material presence.
