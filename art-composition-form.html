Art & Sketching

Composition & Form Studies

2025

Overview

Studies focused on composition through reduction, contrast, and spatial relationships.

Tools

Gouache

Process

Compositions were developed by simplifying real-world forms into geometric structures, focusing on the interaction between positive and negative space. High-contrast black and white was used to emphasize balance, rhythm, and hierarchy.

Reflections

Strengthened my understanding of composition as foundational — how space, rather than form alone, defines visual structure.
