About

Yenlik
Abdis­sattar

I'm a creative director and designer working across editorial, film, illustration, and AI — building a practice that refuses to stay in one lane.

My work starts with observation — in sketches, in cities, in the quiet logic of a colour system — and moves toward something both considered and alive. I use AI not as a shortcut, but as a collaborator.

Currently studying design with a focus on editorial systems, motion, and speculative fashion. Based in Seoul.
